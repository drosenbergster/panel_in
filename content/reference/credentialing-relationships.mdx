# Shared Credentialing Relationships

Several Oregon CCOs share credentialing processes through parent organizations. Understanding these relationships can save significant time — one credential packet may cover multiple CCOs.

## CareOregon Credentialing

**Parent organization:** CareOregon

**Affiliated CCOs:**
- [AllCare CCO](/pathway/allcare) — Curry, Douglas, Jackson, Josephine counties
- [Jackson Care Connect](/pathway/jackson-care-connect) — Jackson County
- [Columbia Pacific CCO](/pathway/columbia-pacific) — Clatsop, Columbia, Tillamook counties

### How It Works

All three CCOs use CareOregon as their credentialing entity. You submit one credential packet to CareOregon, and it may cover all three CCOs.

**What to submit:**
- OPCA (2025 version) with attestation signed within 90 days
- OHP 3108 specifying the CCO(s) you want to join
- Seclusion & Restraint confirmation letter
- Professional liability insurance face sheet
- Supporting documents (license, work history, etc.)

**Submit to:** [credentialing@careoregon.org](mailto:credentialing@careoregon.org)

### Important: Verify Coverage

While one packet may cover multiple CCOs, always **confirm with CareOregon** which CCOs your submission covers. Ask explicitly: "Does this application also credential me with [AllCare / Jackson Care Connect / Columbia Pacific]?"

### Non-Contracted Provider Policy

As of October 2025, CareOregon requires full credentialing before providers can see members of any affiliated CCO. You cannot bill retroactively as a non-contracted provider.

## PacificSource Community Solutions Credentialing

**Parent organization:** PacificSource

**Service areas:**
- [PacificSource Columbia Gorge](/pathway/pacificsource-gorge) — Hood River, Wasco counties
- [PacificSource Marion/Polk](/pathway/pacificsource-marion-polk) — Marion, Polk counties
- [PacificSource Central Oregon](/pathway/pacificsource-central-oregon) — Deschutes, Crook, Jefferson, Klamath counties

### How It Works

PacificSource operates multiple CCO service areas under a shared credentialing process. When enrolling, indicate which service area(s) you want to join.

**What to submit:**
- Oregon Provider Medicaid ID Application
- OPCA or Medicaid Provider Validation Application (as directed)
- Professional liability insurance face sheet
- Supporting documents

**Submit to:** [ORProviderService@PacificSource.com](mailto:ORProviderService@PacificSource.com)

### Multi-Region Enrollment

If you serve patients across multiple PacificSource regions (e.g., both Central Oregon and Marion/Polk), mention this in your initial enrollment submission. One credentialing process may cover all PacificSource service areas.

## Strategic Implications

When planning your credentialing sequence:

1. **Start with CareOregon** if you selected AllCare, JCC, or Columbia Pacific counties — one submission covers up to 3 CCOs
2. **Start with PacificSource** if you selected Gorge, Marion/Polk, or Central Oregon counties — one submission may cover up to 3 service areas
3. **Independent CCOs** require separate applications to each

This is why Panel In groups CareOregon and PacificSource CCOs together on your pathway dashboard — to help you see the efficiency opportunities.
