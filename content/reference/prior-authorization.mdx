# Prior Authorization by CCO

This reference summarizes prior authorization requirements for routine outpatient behavioral health therapy across Oregon CCOs. Most CCOs do **not** require prior authorization for standard outpatient therapy.

## Summary Table

| CCO | Prior Auth Required? | Notes |
|-----|---------------------|-------|
| **EOCCO** | No | No prior auth for routine outpatient therapy |
| **AllCare** | No | CareOregon-affiliated |
| **Jackson Care Connect** | No | CareOregon-affiliated |
| **Columbia Pacific** | No | CareOregon-affiliated, uses CIM BH Portal for other auths |
| **IHN-CCO** | No | Confirmed — no prior auth for routine outpatient |
| **Health Share of Oregon** | No | Depends on health plan partner; generally no auth for outpatient BH |
| **PacificSource (all regions)** | No | Gorge, Marion/Polk, and Central Oregon |
| **Trillium (SW)** | **Yes — after 25 units** | Prior auth required after 25 outpatient therapy units per calendar year. Policy under revision, expected update April 2026 |
| **Trillium (Tri-County)** | **Yes — after 25 units** | Same policy as Trillium SW |
| **Umpqua Health Alliance** | No | No prior auth for routine outpatient therapy |
| **Cascade Health Alliance** | Verify | Contact provider services to confirm current policy |
| **Advanced Health** | Verify | Contact provider services to confirm current policy |
| **Yamhill Community Care** | Verify | Contact provider services to confirm current policy |

## Key Findings

- **7 of 9 major CCOs** do not require prior authorization for routine outpatient behavioral health therapy
- **Trillium** (both Southwest and Tri-County) is the notable exception with a 25-unit annual threshold
- Smaller CCOs (Cascade, Advanced Health, Yamhill) should be verified directly

## Trillium Prior Authorization Details

Trillium Community Health Plan requires prior authorization after **25 outpatient therapy units per calendar year**. This threshold applies to both Trillium Southwest and Trillium Tri-County.

- Units reset each calendar year
- This policy is **under revision** with an expected update in **April 2026**
- Contact Trillium directly to verify the current threshold before it affects your practice

## What "No Prior Auth" Means

"No prior authorization for routine outpatient therapy" means you can:

- Schedule and provide standard therapy sessions (individual, family, group)
- Bill using standard CPT codes (90834, 90837, 90847, 90853)
- Without obtaining advance approval from the CCO

This generally does **not** apply to:

- Intensive outpatient programs (IOP)
- Psychological testing or evaluations beyond standard assessment
- Specialized treatment modalities
- Services exceeding specific unit thresholds (e.g., Trillium)

Always verify with each CCO for non-routine services.
