# Hospital Admission Plan Template

Some CCOs (notably Trillium) require a Hospital Admission Plan as part of the credentialing packet. This one-page letter describes your procedures for handling psychiatric emergencies and hospital referrals.

Below is a ready-to-use template. Print on your practice letterhead, fill in the bracketed fields, sign, and include with your credentialing submission.

---

**[Your Practice Name]**
**[Your Address]**
**[City, State ZIP]**
**[Phone Number]**

**Date:** [Date]

**Re: Hospital Admission Plan**

To Whom It May Concern:

This letter describes the hospital admission procedures for **[Your Name, Credentials]**, practicing at **[Practice Name]** in **[City, Oregon]**.

## Emergency Assessment

When a client presents with acute psychiatric symptoms requiring hospitalization, I follow these procedures:

1. **Immediate safety assessment** — Evaluate for suicidal ideation, homicidal ideation, or psychosis using standardized screening tools
2. **Crisis stabilization** — Attempt de-escalation and safety planning when clinically appropriate
3. **Emergency contact** — If the client requires immediate emergency services, call 911

## Hospital Referral Process

When inpatient psychiatric hospitalization is clinically indicated:

1. Contact the nearest **psychiatric emergency services** or **hospital emergency department**
2. Provide a verbal clinical summary to the receiving facility
3. Facilitate transportation (ambulance, crisis team, or family member as appropriate)
4. Send a written clinical summary within 24 hours of transfer

## Local Emergency Resources

- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text 988
- **Local hospital emergency department:** [Hospital Name, Phone]
- **County crisis line:** [County Crisis Line Number]

## Follow-Up

After a client is hospitalized, I coordinate with the inpatient treatment team regarding:

- Discharge planning and outpatient follow-up
- Medication management coordination
- Safety plan review upon return to outpatient care

Sincerely,

**[Your Name, Credentials]**
**[Oregon License Number]**
**[NPI Number]**

---

## Usage Notes

- Print on your practice letterhead
- Fill in all bracketed fields with your specific information
- Sign and date before including with your credentialing packet
- Keep a copy for your records
- Update local emergency resources as needed
