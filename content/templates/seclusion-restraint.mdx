# Seclusion & Restraint Policy Letter Template

CareOregon-affiliated CCOs (AllCare, Jackson Care Connect, Columbia Pacific) and some other CCOs require a signed confirmation that your practice prohibits seclusion and restraint. This is a standard compliance requirement per OAR 410-141-3590.

For solo outpatient behavioral health providers, this is a brief one-page letter — not a full policy document.

Below is a ready-to-use template. Print on your practice letterhead, fill in the bracketed fields, sign, and include with your credentialing submission.

---

**[Your Practice Name]**
**[Your Address]**
**[City, State ZIP]**
**[Phone Number]**

**Date:** [Date]

**Re: Seclusion and Restraint Policy Confirmation**

To Whom It May Concern:

This letter confirms that **[Your Name, Credentials]**, operating as **[Practice Name]** in **[City, Oregon]**, maintains a practice policy that **prohibits the use of seclusion and restraint** in accordance with OAR 410-141-3590.

## Practice Setting

My practice is a solo outpatient behavioral health practice providing psychotherapy services. The practice setting does not include:

- Inpatient or residential treatment facilities
- Secured or locked treatment environments
- Group home or congregate care settings

## Policy Statement

As an outpatient behavioral health provider:

1. **Seclusion is never used** — No client is involuntarily confined to a room or area
2. **Physical restraint is never used** — No physical holds, mechanical devices, or chemical restraints are applied
3. **De-escalation is the standard** — When a client is in crisis, verbal de-escalation, safety planning, and referral to emergency services are used

## Emergency Protocol

In the event of a psychiatric emergency where a client poses an imminent danger to self or others:

- Emergency services (911) are contacted
- The client is referred to the nearest emergency department
- No seclusion or restraint is used at any point

This policy is reviewed annually and all applicable staff are trained accordingly.

Sincerely,

**[Your Name, Credentials]**
**[Oregon License Number]**
**[NPI Number]**

---

## Usage Notes

- Print on your practice letterhead
- Fill in all bracketed fields with your specific information
- Sign and date before including with your credentialing packet
- Keep a copy for your records
- Required by CareOregon (AllCare, JCC, Columbia Pacific) and Trillium
