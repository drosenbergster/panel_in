# Yamhill Community Care Provider Enrollment

Yamhill Community Care (YCC) serves Yamhill, Washington, and Polk counties. YCC is a smaller CCO and provider enrollment details should be confirmed directly with their office.

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile (completed and attested)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M)

## Step 1: Contact Yamhill Community Care

Contact YCC's provider services to request current enrollment materials and confirm network availability:

**Website:** [yamhillcco.org/providers](https://www.yamhillcco.org/providers)

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 2: Complete Required Forms

Based on your initial contact, YCC will provide their specific enrollment forms. Standard requirements typically include:

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OHP 3108 or CCO-specific enrollment form | As directed by YCC |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |
| W-9 form | Current, signed |

<TimeEstimate minutes={30} />

## Step 3: Submit and Follow Up

Submit your completed packet as directed by YCC's provider services team. Follow up within 2 weeks if you haven't received confirmation.

## County Overlap Note

Washington and Polk counties are also served by other CCOs. If you selected these counties, you may see multiple CCOs in your pathway:

- **Washington County:** Also served by Health Share of Oregon and Trillium Tri-County
- **Polk County:** Also served by PacificSource Marion/Polk

Each requires separate credentialing.

## Timeline

Standard credentialing timeline is **up to 90 days** per ORS 743B.454.
