# Columbia Pacific CCO Provider Enrollment

Columbia Pacific CCO serves Clatsop, Columbia, and Tillamook counties on the Oregon coast. Credentialing is administered by **CareOregon** — the same process as AllCare and Jackson Care Connect. One credential packet may cover all three CCOs.

## Critical: Non-Contracted Provider Policy

As of **October 1, 2025**, CareOregon no longer allows non-contracted behavioral health providers to bill for outpatient mental health and SUD services. You **must** complete full credentialing before seeing any Columbia Pacific members.

## Shared CareOregon Credentialing

Columbia Pacific uses the identical CareOregon credentialing process as [AllCare](/pathway/allcare) and [Jackson Care Connect](/pathway/jackson-care-connect). If you've already submitted a CareOregon credential packet, contact them to confirm whether it also covers Columbia Pacific.

## Required Documents

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OHP 3108 | Provider Enrollment Request — specify Columbia Pacific CCO |
| OPCA (2025 version) | All pages initialed and dated, attestation signed within 90 days |
| Release of Information | Include "All Contracted Health Plans" |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Seclusion & Restraint confirmation letter | On practice letterhead, per OAR 410-141-3590 |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |

## Submit

**Credentialing:** [credentialing@careoregon.org](mailto:credentialing@careoregon.org)

**BH provider data updates:** [BHProviderDataUpdates@careoregon.org](mailto:BHProviderDataUpdates@careoregon.org)

**Phone:** 503-488-2822 or 855-722-8206

## After Enrollment

Columbia Pacific uses CareOregon's provider systems:

- **CIM BH Portal** for authorizations
- **Connect Portal** for provider access and updates

No prior authorization is required for outpatient therapy services.

## Timeline

Up to **90 days** per ORS 743B.454. If you've already submitted for AllCare or Jackson Care Connect, the Columbia Pacific credentialing may be streamlined — confirm with CareOregon.
