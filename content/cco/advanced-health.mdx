# Advanced Health Provider Enrollment

Advanced Health serves Coos and Curry counties on the Southern Oregon coast. Advanced Health is a smaller CCO and provider enrollment details should be confirmed directly with their office.

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile (completed and attested)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M)

## Step 1: Contact Advanced Health

Contact Advanced Health's provider services to request current enrollment materials and confirm network availability:

**Website:** [advancedhealth.com/providers](https://www.advancedhealth.com/providers)

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 2: Complete Required Forms

Based on your initial contact, Advanced Health will provide their specific enrollment forms. Standard requirements typically include:

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OHP 3108 or CCO-specific enrollment form | As directed by Advanced Health |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |
| W-9 form | Current, signed |

<TimeEstimate minutes={30} />

## Step 3: Submit and Follow Up

Submit your completed packet as directed by Advanced Health's provider services team.

## Curry County Overlap

Curry County is also served by [AllCare CCO](/pathway/allcare). If you selected Curry County, both Advanced Health and AllCare will appear in your pathway. AllCare uses CareOregon credentialing, which is a separate process.

## Timeline

Standard credentialing timeline is **up to 90 days** per ORS 743B.454.
