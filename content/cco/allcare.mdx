# AllCare CCO Provider Enrollment

AllCare CCO serves Curry, Douglas, Jackson, and Josephine counties in Southern Oregon. Credentialing is administered by **CareOregon** — the same process covers AllCare, Jackson Care Connect, and Columbia Pacific. One credential packet submitted to CareOregon may cover all three CCOs.

## Critical: Non-Contracted Provider Policy

As of **October 1, 2025**, CareOregon no longer allows non-contracted behavioral health providers to bill for outpatient mental health and SUD services. You **must** complete full credentialing and contracting before seeing any AllCare members. You cannot start seeing patients and credential retroactively.

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile (completed and attested)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M recommended)
- OPCA (2025 version) — Oregon Practitioner Credentialing Application

## Step 1: Complete the OPCA (2025 Version)

Download the current [Oregon Practitioner Credentialing Application (OPCA)](https://www.careoregon.org/docs/default-source/providers/behavioral-health/careoregon-bh-credentialing-checklist.pdf). This is the standard credentialing form used by all CareOregon-affiliated CCOs.

- Initial and date every page
- Complete all sections including work history (mark current positions as "Current")
- **Attestation must be signed within 90 days** of submission — expired attestations are rejected

<TimeEstimate minutes={45} />

## Step 2: Complete the OHP 3108

Download [OHP 3108 (Provider Enrollment Request for CCO Providers)](https://www.oregon.gov/oha/HSD/OHP/Tools/Provider%20Enrollment%20Request%20for%20CCO%20Providers%20OHP%203108%201.2025.pdf). This is the state form that links you to the specific CCO.

<TimeEstimate minutes={15} />

## Step 3: Prepare the Seclusion & Restraint Confirmation

CareOregon-affiliated CCOs require a **one-page confirmation letter** on your practice letterhead stating your practice prohibits seclusion and restraint per OAR 410-141-3590. This is not a full policy document — a signed letter is sufficient.

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 4: Gather Required Documents

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OPCA (2025 version) | All pages initialed and dated, attestation signed within 90 days |
| OHP 3108 | Provider Enrollment Request for CCO Providers |
| Release of Information | Include "All Contracted Health Plans" |
| Work history | All employment, current positions marked "Current" |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Seclusion & Restraint confirmation letter | On practice letterhead, signed |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |

## Step 5: Submit to CareOregon

Send your complete packet to:

**Email:** [credentialing@careoregon.org](mailto:credentialing@careoregon.org)

**Phone:** (541) 471-4106 or (888) 460-0185

## Step 6: Follow Up

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Expected timeline** | Up to 90 days per ORS 743B.454 |
| **Key requirement** | Attestation must be dated within 90 days — re-sign if needed |
| **Shared credentialing** | Ask CareOregon if this packet also covers Jackson Care Connect and Columbia Pacific |
