# EOCCO Provider Enrollment

Eastern Oregon Coordinated Care Organization (EOCCO) is managed by **Moda Health** and covers 12 counties across eastern Oregon — the largest single-CCO coverage area in the state. One application covers Baker, Gilliam, Grant, Harney, Lake, Malheur, Morrow, Sherman, Umatilla, Union, Wallowa, and Wheeler counties.

## Before You Start

You **must** be enrolled with OHA's Division of Medical Assistance Programs (DMAP) before applying to EOCCO. Claims will be denied without an active DMAP registration. If you haven't completed this step, see the [OHA MMIS Enrollment guide](/guides/oha-mmis-enrollment) first.

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active Oregon LCSW license
- National Provider Identifier (NPI) — Type 1 (individual)
- OHA Provider Enrollment Agreement (Form 3975) — completed and submitted
- DMAP registration confirmation (PIN letter from OHA)

## Step 1: Download the Application

Download the EOCCO provider enrollment application from the [EOCCO Become a Provider](https://www.eocco.com/providers/becomeaprovider) page. Look for the OHA managed care provider application specific to EOCCO.

<TimeEstimate minutes={15} />

## Step 2: Complete the Provider Interest Form

EOCCO requires a separate **Behavioral Health Provider Interest Form** for behavioral health providers joining the network. This form is available on the same [Become a Provider](https://www.eocco.com/providers/becomeaprovider) page.

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 3: Gather Required Documents

Assemble these documents for your submission:

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Completed EOCCO application | All fields filled, signed, and dated |
| OHA Provider Enrollment Agreement (Form 3975) | Must be the version you submitted to OHA |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |
| BH Provider Interest Form | Completed in Step 2 |

## Step 4: Submit Your Application

Send your completed application packet to:

**Email:** [ProviderDMAPApps@modahealth.com](mailto:ProviderDMAPApps@modahealth.com)

Include all documents from Step 3 as PDF attachments in a single email. Use a subject line like: "New BH Provider Enrollment — [Your Name], LCSW, NPI [Your NPI]".

## Step 5: Follow Up

After submission, EOCCO/Moda Health will process your application and submit it to OHA for approval.

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Expected timeline** | ~30 days for OHA approval after EOCCO submits |
| **Credentialing contact** | [credentialing@modahealth.com](mailto:credentialing@modahealth.com) |
| **Phone** | 888-788-9821 |

If you haven't heard back within 30 days, contact credentialing at the number above. Have your NPI ready when you call.

## Key Requirements Summary

- **DMAP registration is mandatory** — complete this before applying
- EOCCO is administered by Moda Health; all correspondence goes through Moda
- One application covers all 12 eastern Oregon counties
- No CAQH requirement for EOCCO enrollment
- No Hospital Admit Plan or Seclusion & Restraint Policy required (unlike CareOregon-affiliated CCOs)
