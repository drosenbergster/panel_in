# PacificSource Community Solutions — Central Oregon Provider Enrollment

PacificSource Community Solutions (Central Oregon) serves Deschutes, Crook, Jefferson, and Klamath counties. PacificSource service areas share a credentialing process — if you're also enrolling with PacificSource Columbia Gorge or Marion/Polk, one submission may cover multiple service areas.

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile (completed and attested)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M)

## Step 1: Request a Medicaid Provider ID

Contact PacificSource to request an **Oregon Provider Medicaid ID Application**.

**Email:** [ORProviderService@PacificSource.com](mailto:ORProviderService@PacificSource.com)

**Expected turnaround:** 7–10 business days for Medicaid ID issuance.

<TimeEstimate minutes={15} />

## Step 2: Complete the OPCA or Medicaid Provider Validation Application

Depending on what PacificSource requests, complete either:

- The **OPCA** (Oregon Practitioner Credentialing Application), OR
- The **Medicaid Provider Validation Application** (a PacificSource-specific form)

PacificSource will indicate which form to use when they issue your Medicaid ID.

<TimeEstimate minutes={45} />

## Step 3: Gather Required Documents

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| Oregon Provider Medicaid ID Application | Completed and submitted |
| OPCA or Medicaid Provider Validation Application | As directed by PacificSource |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |

## Step 4: Submit to PacificSource

**Email:** [ORProviderService@PacificSource.com](mailto:ORProviderService@PacificSource.com)

If you're enrolling with multiple PacificSource service areas, mention this in your submission — one credentialing process may cover all service areas.

## Note: Klamath County Overlap

Klamath County is also served by [Cascade Health Alliance](/pathway/cascade). If you selected Klamath County, you may see both PacificSource Central Oregon and Cascade in your pathway. These are separate CCOs with separate credentialing processes.

## Timeline

- **Medicaid ID:** 7–10 business days
- **Full credentialing:** Up to 90 days per ORS 743B.454
