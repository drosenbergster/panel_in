# Trillium Community Health Plan — Tri-County Provider Enrollment

Trillium Community Health Plan (Tri-County) serves Clackamas, Multnomah, and Washington counties in the Portland metro area. Trillium is administered by **Centene Corporation**, so CAQH authorization must name Centene. The credentialing process is the same as [Trillium Southwest](/pathway/trillium-sw).

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile — **authorize Centene Corp** as a participating plan
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M)

## Step 1: Authorize Centene in CAQH

In your CAQH ProView profile, go to **Plan Authorization** and add **Centene Corporation**. Allow 24–48 hours for the authorization to process.

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 2: Complete the OPCA

Download and complete the [Oregon Practitioner Credentialing Application (OPCA)](https://www.trilliumohp.com/providers/join-our-network.html). Initial and date every page. Attestation must be signed within 90 days of submission.

<TimeEstimate minutes={45} />

## Step 3: Prepare the Hospital Admission Plan

Trillium requires a **Hospital Admission Plan** describing your procedures for handling psychiatric emergencies and hospital referrals. This can be a one-page document on your practice letterhead.

<TimeEstimate minutes={20} />

## Step 4: Prepare Seclusion & Restraint Confirmation

Provide a signed letter on your practice letterhead confirming your practice prohibits seclusion and restraint per OAR 410-141-3590.

<TimeEstimate minutes={10} />

## Step 5: Gather Required Documents

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OPCA (current version) | All pages initialed and dated, attestation within 90 days |
| Hospital Admission Plan | On practice letterhead |
| Seclusion & Restraint confirmation | Signed letter on letterhead |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |
| CAQH authorization for Centene Corp | Must be active before submission |

## Step 6: Submit to Trillium

**Email:** [ORProviderExperience@trilliumchp.com](mailto:ORProviderExperience@trilliumchp.com)

## Portland Metro Note

The Tri-County area (Clackamas, Multnomah, Washington) is also served by [Health Share of Oregon](/pathway/health-share). If you selected any of these counties, both Trillium Tri-County and Health Share will appear in your pathway as separate CCOs with separate credentialing.

## Important: Prior Authorization for Outpatient Therapy

Trillium requires **prior authorization after 25 outpatient therapy units per calendar year**. This policy is under revision with an expected update in **April 2026**. Verify the current threshold directly with Trillium.

## Timeline

Standard credentialing timeline is **up to 90 days** per ORS 743B.454.
