# Health Share of Oregon Provider Enrollment

Health Share of Oregon is the largest CCO in the state, serving Clackamas, Multnomah, and Washington counties in the Portland metro area. Health Share contracts with multiple health plan partners for behavioral health services — your credentialing may route through one of their partner plans.

## Before You Start

Confirm you have these ready:

- Active DMAP registration (OHA MMIS enrollment complete)
- Active Oregon LCSW license
- NPI (Type 1, individual)
- CAQH ProView profile (completed and attested)
- Professional liability insurance ($1M/$3M)

## Step 1: Submit Interest Through Health Share

Visit Health Share's provider enrollment page to express interest in joining the network:

**Website:** [healthshareoregon.org/providers/become-a-provider](https://www.healthshareoregon.org/providers/become-a-provider)

Health Share will direct you to the appropriate health plan partner based on your location and specialty.

<TimeEstimate minutes={15} />

## Step 2: Complete Credentialing with Assigned Health Plan Partner

Health Share works through partner health plans for behavioral health credentialing. You may be directed to credential through:

- **CareOregon** (one of Health Share's major BH partners)
- Another delegated health plan

The specific forms and process will depend on which partner you're assigned to.

<TimeEstimate minutes={45} />

## Step 3: Standard Document Requirements

Regardless of which partner you credential through, have these ready:

| Document | Notes |
|----------|-------|
| OPCA or partner-specific application | As directed by assigned health plan |
| Professional liability insurance face sheet | Current, showing $1M/$3M coverage |
| Copy of active Oregon LCSW license | Current, unencumbered |
| CAQH ProView profile | Completed and attested |
| W-9 form | Current, signed |

## Portland Metro Note

The Portland metro counties (Clackamas, Multnomah, Washington) are also served by [Trillium Tri-County](/pathway/trillium-tri-county). If you selected any of these counties, both Health Share and Trillium Tri-County will appear in your pathway. They require separate credentialing.

## Timeline

Standard credentialing timeline is **up to 90 days** per ORS 743B.454. Actual timeline depends on which health plan partner manages your credentialing.
